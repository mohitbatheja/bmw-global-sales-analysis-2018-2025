# 🚗 BMW Global Sales Analysis (2018–2025)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Data Analysis** of the **BMW Global Sales (2018–2025)** dataset using **Python**. The primary objective is to analyze sales performance, identify market trends, evaluate regional performance, and generate actionable business insights through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and data visualization.

The project demonstrates the complete Data Analytics workflow, including data cleaning, preprocessing, outlier detection, exploratory analysis, and business insight generation.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze BMW global sales performance from **2018–2025**
- Identify yearly and monthly sales trends
- Calculate yearly revenue growth
- Compare sales performance across different regions
- Analyze EV (BEV) adoption trends
- Identify top-selling BMW models
- Compare regional pricing strategies
- Generate business insights using visualizations

# 📊 Business Questions Answered

### 1. Year-wise Units Sold Trend

Analyzed annual units sold to identify sales growth patterns.

### 2. Month-wise Sales Trend

Performed seasonality analysis to identify high and low sales months.

### 3. Yearly Revenue Growth Rate

Calculated yearly revenue growth to measure business performance.

### 4. Peak Sales Months

Identified peak sales months using Year and Month analysis.

### 5. Units Sold vs Revenue Relationship

Analyzed the relationship between units sold and total revenue.

### 6. Region-wise Units Sold

Compared units sold across different regions.

### 7. Region-wise Revenue Distribution

Analyzed revenue contribution by each region.

### 8. Region-wise Average Price Comparison

Compared average vehicle prices across regions.

### 9. EV (BEV Share) Growth Analysis

Identified regions with the fastest electric vehicle adoption.

### 10. Top Selling BMW Models

Ranked BMW models based on units sold.

### 11. Highest Revenue Generating Models

Identified models generating the highest revenue.

### 12. Region-wise Price Variation

Compared pricing differences across global markets.
